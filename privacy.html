Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026 — Trading Signals Hub ("we", "our").

What we collect

  • Account data: name and email if you create an account in our app.
  • Device data: a device identifier and push-notification token so we can deliver signal alerts to your phone.
  • Payment proof: if you subscribe, the payment screenshot/transaction reference you submit for verification.
  • Usage basics: which plan you're on, trial status, and notification preferences.

What we do NOT do

  • We do not sell your personal data to anyone.
  • We do not read your photo library — images you send us are only the ones you pick yourself.
  • We do not collect financial account details; payments are made directly by you on third-party exchanges/wallets.

How data is used

To deliver signal notifications, manage your trial/subscription, verify payments, provide support, and (if you joined through a partner) credit that partner's referral.

Third-party services

We use Google Firebase (push notifications), Supabase (secure database hosting), Discord and WhatsApp (community delivery). Each has its own privacy policy. This landing page is hosted on GitHub Pages; standard server logs may apply.

Data deletion

You can request deletion of your account and data anytime by contacting us in the app (Settings → Help & Support) or via our Discord support ticket. We delete within 30 days.

Children

Our services are not directed at persons under 18.

Contact

Questions? Open a support ticket in our Discord server or reach us through the app's Help & Support section.
